<p><strong>The Inkjet Marking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CIJ</li><li>DOD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet marking system market includes two primary types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop-On-Demand (DOD) technologies. CIJ systems continuously eject ink droplets, making them suitable for high-speed production and large-scale printing applications. Conversely, DOD technology releases ink droplets only when needed, allowing for precise control and detailed printing on various surfaces. Both types are widely used in industrial applications for coding, labeling, and marking, each offering distinct advantages in speed, precision, and versatility depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
