<p><p>A hydraulic damper is a device that uses hydraulic fluid to absorb and dissipate energy, typically in the form of vibrations or shocks. It is commonly used in various applications such as automotive suspensions, industrial machinery, and aerospace systems to enhance stability and comfort by controlling motion. The hydraulic damper operates by converting kinetic energy into thermal energy, effectively reducing the amplitude of oscillations.</p><p>The Hydraulic Damper Market is anticipated to grow significantly, expecting a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.
