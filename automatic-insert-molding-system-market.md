<p><p>The Automatic Insert Molding System market encompasses several specialized techniques, including Multi-Component Injection Molding, Multi-Shot Injection Molding, and Overmolding. Multi-Component Injection Molding combines different materials within a single mold to create complex parts. Multi-Shot Injection Molding allows for sequential layering of materials to enhance functionality or aesthetics in a single process. Overmolding involves adding a second material over a substrate to improve grip, ergonomics, or sealing. These methods are crucial for producing advanced, multifunctional components in various industries.</p></p>
