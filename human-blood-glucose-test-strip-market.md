<p><p>The human blood glucose test strip market is categorized into four main types based on the enzyme technology used. Glucose oxidase strips employ a traditional method that generates a color change in the presence of glucose. PQQ-glucose dehydrogenase strips offer improved accuracy and stability through a coenzyme, PQQ. FAD-glucose dehydrogenase strips enhance sensitivity and reduce interference, while hexokinase strips utilize a different reaction mechanism for precise readings. Each type caters to specific user needs and technological advancements in blood glucose monitoring.</p></p>
