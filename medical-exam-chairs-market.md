<p><p>The medical exam chairs market comprises primarily two types: manual and electrical exam chairs. Manual exam chairs require manual adjustments for positioning, offering simplicity and reliability, often preferred in smaller clinics where budget constraints exist. In contrast, electrical exam chairs feature motorized controls for height and positioning adjustments, enhancing patient comfort and accessibility for practitioners. These chairs are more commonly used in hospitals and larger facilities due to their efficiency and ease of use during various medical examinations and procedures.</p></p>
