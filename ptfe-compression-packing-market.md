<p><p>The PTFE compression packing market is divided into two main types: without lubrication and lubrication type. Without lubrication type is designed for applications where minimal friction is required, ideal for dry or high-temperature environments. Conversely, lubrication type packing incorporates an external lubricant to enhance wear resistance and reduce friction, making it suitable for fluid handling in pumps and valves. Both types cater to specific operational needs, emphasizing durability and performance across diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
