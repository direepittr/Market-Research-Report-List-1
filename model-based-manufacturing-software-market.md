<p><p>Model-Based Manufacturing Software (MBMS) can be categorized into two main types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises software is installed locally on a company’s servers, offering greater control, customization, and security, but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers, providing greater accessibility, scalability, and ease of updates, while reducing the need for extensive IT infrastructure. Both models cater to diverse manufacturing needs, balancing flexibility and control to optimize production processes.</p></p>
