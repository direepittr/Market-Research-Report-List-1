<p><p>The Hospital Linen Supply and Management Services market comprises two primary types: Rental Systems and Customer Owned Goods. </p><p>In a Rental System, healthcare facilities lease linens from specialized service providers, ensuring a consistent supply of clean, high-quality textiles while outsourcing laundry and maintenance tasks. Conversely, the Customer Owned Goods market involves hospitals managing their own linen inventory, where they purchase and launder linens internally. This approach offers greater control over quality and availability but requires more management resources. Both systems aim to enhance operational efficiency and patient care.</p></p>
