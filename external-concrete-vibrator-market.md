<p><p>The external concrete vibrator market can be categorized into three frequency types: high frequency, medium frequency, and low frequency. High-frequency vibrators operate at 9,000 RPM or more, effectively consolidating concrete for critical applications. Medium-frequency vibrators, typically running between 3,500 to 9,000 RPM, provide a balanced performance suitable for general construction tasks. Low-frequency vibrators, operating below 3,500 RPM, are ideal for heavier mixtures and larger projects where deeper penetration and higher force are required to eliminate air pockets.</p></p>
