<p><strong>The Nuclear Waste Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Level Waste</li><li>Intermediate Level Waste</li><li>High Level Waste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear waste management system market is categorized into three main types based on waste radioactivity levels. Low Level Waste (LLW) includes materials with slight radioactivity, primarily from medical and industrial use, requiring minimal shielding during disposal. Intermediate Level Waste (ILW) has higher radioactivity, necessitating more robust containment, often requiring disposal in deep geological formations. High Level Waste (HLW) originates from nuclear reactors and reprocessed fuel, presenting significant hazards, demanding advanced technologies for safe storage and long-term isolation, often entailing extensive regulatory oversight.</p></p>
