<p><strong>The Automotive Turbocharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Turbocharging</li><li>Exhaust Gas Turbocharging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive turbocharger market is categorized primarily into mechanical turbocharging and exhaust gas turbocharging. Mechanical turbocharging utilizes a belt-driven supercharger to compress air into the engine, enhancing performance and efficiency, especially in low RPM scenarios. Conversely, exhaust gas turbocharging harnesses exhaust gases to drive a turbine, forcing more air into the combustion chamber. This method improves engine power and fuel efficiency while reducing emissions, making it a popular choice in modern automotive applications for optimizing performance and sustainability.</p></p>
