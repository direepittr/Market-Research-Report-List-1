<p><p>The sewer drain cable market consists of two main types: Inner Core and No Core cables. Inner Core cables feature a central steel wire, providing increased durability and strength, making them suitable for tackling tough clogs in larger pipes. In contrast, No Core cables are lightweight and flexible, designed for lighter tasks and smaller drain lines, allowing for easier maneuverability in tight spaces. Both types cater to different needs in drain cleaning and maintenance applications.</p></p>
