<p><p>The Low Pressure UV Curing System market includes three primary types: Spot Cure, Flood Cure, and Focused Beam. Spot Cure systems deliver concentrated UV light to specific areas, ideal for small or intricate applications. Flood Cure systems provide broad-spectrum UV exposure, effectively curing larger surfaces or multiple items simultaneously. Focused Beam systems utilize a directed UV light path for precision curing, suitable for specialized tasks that require accuracy while minimizing surrounding exposure. Each type offers unique advantages for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
