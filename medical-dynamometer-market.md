<p><ul><li>Electronic Medical Dynamometer</li><li>Mechanical Medical Dynamometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical dynamometer market is categorized into two main types: electronic and mechanical dynamometers. Electronic medical dynamometers utilize digital sensors to measure muscle strength and provide precise data output, often featuring advanced functionalities like data storage and interface capabilities for patient tracking. In contrast, mechanical medical dynamometers operate through traditional mechanical components, relying on spring or hydraulic systems to gauge force exerted by muscles. Both types are essential for rehabilitation and strength assessment in clinical settings, each catering to specific user needs.</p></p>
