<p><strong>The Handheld Veterinary RFID Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> High Frequency</li><li>Low Frequency </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld veterinary RFID readers market comprises two main types: high frequency (HF) and low frequency (LF) readers. HF readers operate at 13.56 MHz, offering faster data transfer and a read range of up to 10 centimeters, ideal for applications like tracking pet microchip data. Conversely, LF readers, typically operating at 125-134 kHz, provide longer read ranges of up to 10 centimeters but with slower data transfer, making them suitable for reading larger animal tags in veterinary settings.</p></p>
