<p><p>The Peripheral Nerve Block Needle Market is segmented into two primary types: Ultrasound Guidance and Electrostimulation. </p><p>Ultrasound Guidance utilizes real-time imaging to precisely locate nerves, enhancing accuracy and safety during nerve block procedures. This technology improves patient outcomes and reduces complications. </p><p>Electrostimulation, on the other hand, involves the application of electrical impulses to identify nerve pathways. This technique aids in ensuring correct needle placement, minimizing the risk of nerve damage and improving analgesia efficacy. Both methods are crucial in advancing regional anesthesia practices.</p></p>
