<p><strong>The VOC Gas Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC gas monitor market features various technologies, primarily including Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductors (MOS). PID monitors utilize ultraviolet light to detect volatile organic compounds, offering high sensitivity and a wide detection range, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, MOS sensors operate based on the change in resistance of metal-oxide materials in the presence of VOCs, providing cost-effective and compact solutions suitable for residential or low-concentration environments. Both technologies cater to specific monitoring needs.</p></p>
