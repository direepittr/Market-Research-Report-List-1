<p><strong>The Water Electrolysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electroliser</li><li>PEM Electroliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water electrolysis market comprises two main types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and proton exchange membrane (PEM) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline electrolyte solution, offering low initial costs and proven durability, making them suitable for large-scale hydrogen production. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers employ a solid polymer membrane, enabling higher efficiency, compact design, and faster response times, ideal for applications requiring dynamic operation. Both technologies cater to the growing demand for green hydrogen in various industries.</p></p>
