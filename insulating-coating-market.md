<p><p>The insulating coating market comprises several types, each offering unique properties. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) coatings are known for their low friction and high-temperature resistance, ideal for non-stick surfaces. Ceramic coatings offer exceptional thermal resistance and durability, enhancing the lifespan of components.
