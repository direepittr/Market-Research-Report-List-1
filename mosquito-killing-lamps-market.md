<p><p>The mosquito killing lamps market features several types designed to attract and eliminate mosquitoes. Electric shock type lamps use high voltage grids to kill insects on contact. Sticky trap type lamps attract mosquitoes using light and then trap them on adhesive surfaces. Airflow type lamps create a vacuum to draw mosquitoes into a container where they cannot escape. Photocatalysis type lamps utilize light-activated reactions to produce attractants, drawing mosquitoes in for capture or destruction. Each type offers unique advantages for pest control.</p></p>
