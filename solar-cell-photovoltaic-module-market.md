<p><strong>The Solar Cell (Photovoltaic) Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mono-Si Modules</li><li>Multi-Si Modules</li><li>CdTe Modules</li><li>CIGS Modules</li><li>a-Si Modules</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar cell market comprises various types of photovoltaic modules. Mono-Si modules, made from single-crystal silicon, offer high efficiency and performance. Multi-Si modules, derived from multiple silicon crystals, are cost-effective but slightly less efficient. CdTe modules utilize cadmium telluride, providing affordability and lower environmental impact. CIGS modules consist of copper indium gallium selenide for flexible applications and good efficiency. a-Si modules utilize amorphous silicon, ideal for low-light conditions. Other modules may include emerging technologies and hybrid solutions that enhance performance and versatility.</p></p>
