<p><ul><li>One-way Reverse Osmosis</li><li>Two-way Reverse Osmosis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seawater reverse osmosis (SWRO) system market is categorized into two primary types: one-way reverse osmosis and two-way reverse osmosis. One-way reverse osmosis systems are designed for straightforward desalination processes, effectively filtering seawater to produce potable water without recirculating waste. In contrast, two-way reverse osmosis systems incorporate a more complex mechanism that allows for the recirculation of some of the concentrated brine, improving energy efficiency and reducing waste, ultimately enhancing sustainable water supply solutions.</p></p>
