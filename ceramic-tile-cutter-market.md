<p><p>The ceramic tile cutter market includes two main types: wet tile cutters and dry tile cutters. Wet tile cutters utilize water to cool the blade and minimize dust, offering precision and cleaner cuts, making them ideal for larger tiles. In contrast, dry tile cutters operate without water, utilizing specialized blades designed to create minimal dust and provide fast, efficient cuts, suitable for smaller projects or quick jobs. Both types cater to different needs in tile installation and renovation, enhancing versatility in tile cutting tasks.</p></p>
