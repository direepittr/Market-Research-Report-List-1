<p><strong>The CPU Heatsink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Cooling CPU Heatsink</li><li>Water Cooling CPU Heatsink</li><li>Thermoelectric Cooling CPU Heatsink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPU heatsink market encompasses three primary types: air cooling, water cooling, and thermoelectric cooling. Air cooling heatsinks use metal fins and fans to dissipate heat, offering cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. Water cooling solutions employ liquid to transfer heat away from the CPU, providing superior cooling efficiency, ideal for high-performance systems. Thermoelectric cooling heatsinks utilize thermoelectric modules to create a temperature difference, offering advanced performance but at a higher cost, often used in specialized applications.</p></p>
