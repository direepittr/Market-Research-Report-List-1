<p><ul><li>Standard HPLC Pump</li><li>UHPLC Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HPLC pumps market is divided into two main types: Standard HPLC pumps and UHPLC pumps. Standard HPLC pumps are designed for conventional high-performance liquid chromatography applications, offering reliable performance and decent pressure capabilities. In contrast, UHPLC pumps are engineered for ultra-high-performance liquid chromatography, delivering higher pressures and improved resolution, thus supporting faster analysis and more efficient separations. Both types serve distinct applications in laboratories, catering to varying needs in terms of speed, sensitivity, and performance in analytical workflows.</p></p>
