The primary market types include BOE 6:1 and BOE 7:1, which indicate the ratio of ammonium fluoride to hydrofluoric acid. BOE 6:1 is preferred for applications requiring moderate etching rates, while BOE 7:1 offers slower etching for finer control. Other market variants may include different ratios or formulations tailored to specific semiconductor processes.</p></p>
