<p><ul><li>Cancellous Bone Screw</li><li>Cortical Bone Screw</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Fixation Screw market is categorized into two primary types: cancellous bone screws and cortical bone screws. Cancellous bone screws are designed for securing soft, spongy bone, typically used in orthopedic surgeries involving the pelvis, spine, and joints. Cortical bone screws, on the other hand, are meant for dense, outer bone layers, providing stable fixation in applications such as fracture repair and osteosynthesis. Additionally, the "other" market encompasses specialized screws for unique bone conditions or surgical procedures.</p></p>
