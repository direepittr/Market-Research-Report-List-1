<p><p>The Visual Inspection Equipment market encompasses two primary types: Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and Remote Visual Inspection (RVI). Non-Destructive Testing involves evaluating materials and components without causing damage, utilizing techniques such as ultrasonic or radiographic methods to detect flaws and ensure safety and integrity. Remote Visual Inspection employs cameras and probes to inspect hard-to-reach or hazardous areas, allowing for real-time monitoring and assessment without physical intervention. Both types are essential for quality control and maintenance across various industries, including manufacturing and aerospace.</p></p>
