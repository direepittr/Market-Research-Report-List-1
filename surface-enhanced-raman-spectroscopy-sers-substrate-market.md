<p><ul><li>Precious Metal Sol</li><li>Metal Island Membrane Substrate</li><li>Rough Electrode</li><li>Solid Nanoparticles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS) substrate market includes several types. Precious metal sol substrates utilize colloidal solutions of noble metals to enhance Raman signals. Metal island membrane substrates consist of thin metal films with discontinuities that aid in signal amplification. Rough electrodes have textured surfaces that increase the roughness, maximizing light interaction and enhancing signal intensity. Solid nanoparticles are discrete metal particles that provide local electromagnetic fields, significantly boosting the Raman response for enhanced analytical capabilities in various applications.</p></p>
