<p><p>Laser marking heads are classified into three main types: Semiconductor Laser Marking Heads, CO2 Laser Marking Heads, and Fiber Laser Marking Heads. Semiconductor laser marking heads are compact and used for marking plastics and thin materials. CO2 laser marking heads excel in marking non-metallic materials like wood and glass but are less effective on metals. Fiber laser marking heads offer high precision and efficiency, making them ideal for marking metals and are increasingly favored in industrial applications for their durability and low maintenance.</p></p>
