<p><p>Semi-automatic bagging machines are categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical bagging machines. Horizontal bagging machines are designed to fill and seal bags that are positioned horizontally, making them ideal for bulk items and larger packaging formats. In contrast, vertical bagging machines operate with a vertical orientation, suitable for smaller, lightweight products that require precision filling. Both types enhance efficiency in packaging operations, catering to different product dimensions and industry needs, ultimately streamlining the bagging process.</p></p>
