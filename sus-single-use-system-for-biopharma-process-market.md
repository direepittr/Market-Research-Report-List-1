<p><p>Single Use Systems (SUS) for biopharma processes refer to disposable components used in the manufacture of biopharmaceuticals, such as bags, tubes, and connectors. The rising demand for biopharmaceutical products, driven by advancements in biologics, personalized medicine, and gene therapy, is fueling the growth of the SUS market.</p><p>The SUS for biopharma process market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period. Innovations in materials and technology are also enhancing the functionality and versatility of single-use systems. Trends indicate an increasing adoption of integrated systems that combine various capabilities, such as mixing and storage, which streamline bioprocessing workflows. Furthermore, the growing emphasis on reducing production costs and timelines is prompting biopharma companies to transition to single-use technologies. As regulatory agencies continue to support the use of these systems, the market is likely to see expanded applications across upstream and downstream processes. This growth reflects a broader shift towards more flexible, efficient, and environmentally sustainable biomanufacturing practices.</p></p>
