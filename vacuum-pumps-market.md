<p><p>The vacuum pumps market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Dry vacuum pumps operate without lubricant, making them ideal for applications requiring contamination-free environments. Liquid ring vacuum pumps utilize a liquid as a sealing mechanism, providing efficient gas removal in various industrial processes. Rotary vane vacuum pumps use rotating vanes within a chamber to create a vacuum, known for their reliability and versatility. Each type plays a crucial role in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and manufacturing.</p></p>
