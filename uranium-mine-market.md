<p><ul><li>In Situ Leach Mining (ISL)</li><li>Open-pit Mining</li><li>Underground Mining</li><li>By-product</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uranium mine market consists of various extraction methods, each with distinct characteristics. In Situ Leach Mining (ISL) involves dissolving uranium from underground deposits without significant surface disturbance. Open-pit mining extracts uranium ore from large surface pits, making it suitable for high-grade ores. Underground mining involves deep excavation to access uranium deposits, typically used for lower-grade ores. The by-product market includes uranium recovered as a secondary product from mining other minerals, contributing to overall production efficiency and profitability.</p></p>
