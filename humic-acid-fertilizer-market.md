<p><p>The humic acid fertilizer market is divided into two primary types: solid and liquid. Solid humic acid fertilizers, often in granular or powdered forms, are typically used for soil application, improving soil structure and nutrient retention. Liquid humic acid fertilizers, on the other hand, are designed for foliar application and can be easily mixed with other nutrients, enhancing plant absorption.
