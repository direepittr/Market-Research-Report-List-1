<p><p>The nickel electroplating additive market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Pretreatment Agents**: These are chemicals used to prepare surfaces for electroplating, improving adhesion and uniformity.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Electroplating Additives**: These substances are added to the plating bath to enhance the quality and properties of nickel deposits, influencing factors like brightness and corrosion resistance.</p><p>3. **Post-treatment Agents**: After electroplating, these agents are applied to further enhance the surface characteristics, providing improved finish and durability. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall electroplating process.</p></p>
