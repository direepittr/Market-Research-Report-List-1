<p><p>The UV curable sealant market is divided into rigid and flexible sealants, each serving distinct applications. Rigid sealants offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them ideal for structural bonding in automotive and construction sectors. In contrast, flexible sealants provide elasticity and movement accommodation, suitable for applications in electronics, textiles, and consumer goods where joint movement is common. Both types benefit from rapid curing under UV light, enhancing production efficiency and expanding their usability across various industries.</p></p>
