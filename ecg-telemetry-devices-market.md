<p><p>The ECG telemetry devices market comprises several key types: Resting ECG devices, Stress ECG devices, and Holter monitors. Resting ECG devices capture the heart's electrical activity in a stationary state, primarily for diagnostic purposes. Stress ECG devices monitor heart performance during physical exertion, often used in exercise testing. Holter monitors are portable devices worn for 24 hours or more, providing continuous heart rhythm tracking to detect irregularities over an extended period. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in cardiovascular health monitoring.</p></p>
