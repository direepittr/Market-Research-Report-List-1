<p><p>Silicon photonics merges silicon-based electronics with photonic technologies, enhancing performance in high-performance computing and telecommunications. Optical modulators convert electrical signals to optical ones, enabling faster data transmission. Multiplexers combine multiple signals into a single optical channel, optimizing bandwidth. Photonic ICs integrate various optical components for compact functionality. Waveguides direct light within circuits, minimizing losses. Photodetectors convert light back to electrical signals, crucial for data reception. Other components may include optical amplifiers and sensors, all contributing to efficient optical networking.</p></p>
