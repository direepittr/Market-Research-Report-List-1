<p><p>The artificial heart-lung machine market is divided into two main types: single roller pump heart-lung machines (HLM) and double roller pump heart-lung machines (HLM). Single roller pump HLMs use a single roller to propel blood, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, double roller pump HLMs feature two rollers, providing higher flow rates and better blood handling capabilities, which can enhance patient outcomes during cardiopulmonary bypass surgeries. Both types serve critical roles in surgical settings, ensuring effective oxygenation and circulation.</p></p>
