<p><p>The foamed polyurethane market is categorized into three main types: flexible foam, spray foam, and rigid foam. Flexible foam is commonly used in furniture, mattresses, and automotive applications due to its soft texture and comfort. Spray foam serves as an effective insulation material, providing air sealing and thermal resistance. Rigid foam, characterized by its hard structure, is ideal for construction and industrial uses, offering durability and excellent insulation properties. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance characteristics.</p></p>
