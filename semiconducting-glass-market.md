<p><strong>The Semiconducting Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Type</li><li>Positive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconducting glass market can be divided into two primary types: negative type and positive type. Negative-type semiconducting glass exhibits increased resistivity upon exposure to light or heat, making it suitable for applications requiring photoconductive properties. In contrast, positive-type semiconducting glass shows decreased resistivity when stimulated, allowing it to effectively conduct electrical current in response to contact or thermal energy. Both types play critical roles in various electronic applications, including sensors, displays, and photovoltaic devices, depending on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
