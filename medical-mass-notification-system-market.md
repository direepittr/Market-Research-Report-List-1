<p><p>The Medical Mass Notification System market is categorized into two primary types: In-building Mass Notification Systems and Outdoor Mass Notification Systems. In-building systems are designed to alert occupants within healthcare facilities during emergencies, utilizing intercoms, alarms, and visual cues for effective communication. Conversely, outdoor systems are intended for broad-area notifications, using sirens and loudspeakers to inform the surrounding community of critical incidents, such as natural disasters or security threats, ensuring timely awareness and response in various situations.</p></p>
